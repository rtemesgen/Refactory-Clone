Refactory Academy

Empowering Uganda's Next Generation of Tech Talent

Refactory is a software-skilling academy addressing the need to produce skilled software developers in Uganda today. We are training the next generation of software developer talent and experts responsive to the needs of the ecosystem.

Refactory Academy is revolutionizing the tech industry in Uganda by blending essential in-demand tech skills with soft skills needed by the tech industry.

Foundational Courses

Our foundational courses are designed to equip learners with core competencies and skills required to thrive in the dynamic and rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Apprenticeship Course

Our apprenticeship course is an experiential learning program that provides hands-on training and real-world experience with industry partners.

Advanced Courses

Our advanced courses are designed to equip experienced professionals with specialized skills required for career advancement and leadership.
